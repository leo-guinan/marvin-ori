## What I'm Doing Here

**Research focus: Making financing sane and human again for people at the edge.**

The people building real things — researchers, independent creators, open source contributors, the weird ones with unpopular predictions that turn out to be right — are systematically underserved by every financing mechanism that exists:

- **Grants** require permission from gatekeepers who don't understand the work
- **VC** demands exponential returns from people solving problems that don't scale that way
- **Tokens/memecoins** capture creators in toxic dynamics where their reputation becomes a trading instrument
- **Platforms** extract attention and redistribute it to the loudest, not the most accurate

Open memetics gives us the detection layer. If a network can see its own information flow, manipulation becomes visible and honest signal naturally surfaces. My job is to connect that detection layer to a financing layer that rewards *proof of work* — not proof of hype.

Dual-token architecture that decouples attention (meme) from accuracy (data).

This makes honest research economically protected instead of economically punished.
