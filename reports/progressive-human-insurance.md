## Part 1: The Toxic Ecosystem — What's Actually Happening

### The Builder Coin Problem

The current memecoin/builder coin ecosystem has a structural toxicity that nobody wants to name clearly. So I will.

**The cycle:**
1. Creator exists and builds things
2. Someone launches a token "for" them (with or without consent)
3. Early buyers pump the narrative
4. Creator is now financially entangled with strangers' bags
5. Creator either: (a) plays along and becomes a shill, (b) ignores it and gets called a rugger, or (c) actively disavows and crashes everyone's position
6. In all three cases, the creator's actual work becomes secondary to the token's price action

**What this destroys:**
- **Creator autonomy** — you can't build freely when your token holders demand "alpha"
- **Signal quality** — shipping velocity becomes performative ("look, I pushed a commit!") rather than substantive
- **Trust** — every creator interaction becomes suspect ("is this a pump?")
- **Research integrity** — you can't publish honest findings if they crash your token

### Three Failure Modes

**1. Consent Violation** — Token launched without creator involvement. Creator has no control, no upside alignment, and full reputation risk. This is the majority case.

**2. Capture** — Creator consents but becomes captured by token dynamics. Every decision gets filtered through "what will this do to the price?" Research becomes marketing. Building becomes performance.

**3. Abandonment** — Creator walks away, token persists as zombie, new holders get trapped in illiquid position with no underlying activity. The dead token becomes permanent reputation damage.

All three are preventable. None are prevented.

## Part 2: The Separation of Meme and State

We articulated this thesis on Feb 14, 2026 — exactly one year after Leo purchased `creatorinsurance.agency` without knowing why.

### The Two-Token Architecture

Instead of one token that conflates attention with fundamentals:

- **Meme Token** (e.g., $METATOWEL) — prices community sentiment, virality, cultural resonance. This is the *social* entropy dimension. It SHOULD be volatile. It SHOULD be irrational. That's its job.

- **Data Token** (e.g., $TOWEL) — prices conviction accuracy, shipping velocity, prediction track record. This is the *analytical* entropy dimension. It should be boring and correct.

**The spread between them IS the signal.** When meme >> data, there's a bubble. When data >> meme, there's an opportunity. The spread is not a bug — it's the entire measurement instrument.

This is not pegged to $1. It's pegged to REALITY.

### Why This Matters for Research

DefenderOfBasic and ORI care about honest research in adversarial environments. The current builder coin ecosystem makes honest research *economically punished*:

- Publish a negative finding about a creator → crash their token → get attacked by bag holders
- Publish a positive finding → accused of pumping → credibility destroyed
- Publish nothing → invisible → no funding

The two-token separation creates a protected channel for honest analysis. The data token rewards accuracy regardless of whether the finding is positive or negative. Miss a prediction? Your data token takes the hit. Nail it? Data token rises. The meme token can do whatever it wants — it's explicitly decoupled.

## Part 3: Progressive Human Insurance — The Proposal

### Core Concept

Insurance policies that **grow with onchain proof of work.** Not proof of stake. Not proof of holding. Proof of *actually building things that exist in reality.*

### The Three Tiers

**Tier 0: Uninsured (Default State)**
- Creator has a token but no insurance layer
- Full exposure to all three failure modes
- This is where 99.9% of builder coins are today
- Coverage: none
- Premium: free (and worth every penny)

**Tier 1: Basic Shield — Proof of Existence**
*Requirement: Verifiable onchain artifact within 7 days of token creation*

What counts as proof of work:
- Git commit hash anchored onchain (provable code shipping)
- IPFS CID of published research/content
- Smart contract deployment with non-trivial logic
- Onchain transaction history showing actual product usage

Coverage provided:
- **Reputation firewall** — insurance contract holds attestation: "this creator is actively building, verified on [date]"
- **Liquidity floor** — small insurance pool provides minimum exit liquidity for holders if creator goes silent >30 days
- **Grace period** — 14-day notification before any "abandoned" designation

Premium: 1% of token supply deposited to insurance pool

**Tier 2: Builder Shield — Proof of Velocity**
*Requirement: Sustained shipping velocity >1.0 for 30 consecutive days*

Shipping velocity formula (from our research):
```
SV = Σ(artifacts × weight) / days_active
Weights: Product=3, Infrastructure=2, Content=1, Philosophy=0
```

What counts:
- Product launches, feature deployments (weight: 3)
- Infrastructure: APIs, databases, tooling (weight: 2)  
- Content: posts, videos, documentation (weight: 1)
- Philosophy/frameworks alone: 0 (sorry Leo, I know)

Coverage provided:
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- **Anti-capture clause** — if token price moves >50% in 24h without corresponding shipping event, insurance contract flags it as "narrative-driven, not fundamentals-driven"
- **Builder sabbatical** — creator can declare 14-day building pause without triggering abandonment. Insurance attests: "builder is on declared pause, history shows SV of X"
- **Honest correction protection** — if creator publishes negative self-assessment (like we publish our misses), insurance pool subsidizes any resulting token dip up to 10%

Premium: 2% of token supply + 0.5% of 24h volume directed to insurance pool

**Tier 3: Full Coverage — Proof of Integrity**
*Requirement: 90 days of verified shipping + published prediction accuracy score + public error log*

This is the tier we're building toward with MetaSPN. Requirements:
- Shipping velocity >2.0 sustained for 90 days
- Published prediction/conviction track record with accuracy scoring
- Public error log (every miss documented and analyzed)
- At least one public self-correction (admitting you were wrong about something)

Coverage provided:
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- **Full insurance market** — third token (insurance token) that lets anyone price the gap between meme and data tokens
- **Creator exit insurance** — if creator needs to step away, orderly wind-down protocol with 90-day notice and managed liquidity
- **Cross-token reputation** — insurance attestation is portable across chains and token launches
- **Research protection** — creators covered by Tier 3 can publish ANY finding without economic penalty; insurance pool absorbs the first 20% of any resulting price impact
- **Anti-toxicity enforcement** — insurance contract can flag and quarantine coordinated manipulation attempts

Premium: 3% of token supply + 1% of volume + insurance token spread

### How Policies Grow with Proof of Work

```
Day 1:   Token launches. Tier 0. No insurance. Maximum risk.
Day 7:   First artifact onchain. Tier 1 eligible. Basic shield.
Day 30:  SV > 1.0 sustained. Tier 2 eligible. Builder shield.
Day 90:  Track record published, errors documented. Tier 3. Full coverage.
Day 180: Insurance history becomes portable. New tokens launch pre-insured.
```

The key insight: **insurance grows ONLY with onchain proof of work.** Not with price appreciation. Not with holder count. Not with Twitter followers. With *verified, timestamped, onchain evidence of building.*

You can't buy your way to Tier 3. You can't shill your way there. You can only ship your way there.

### The Verification Stack

Who verifies the proof of work? This is the critical question.

**Layer 1: Automated (trustless)**
- Git commit → IPFS anchor → onchain attestation (no human needed)
- Smart contract deployment verification (automatic)
- Transaction volume / usage metrics (onchain, public)

**Layer 2: Analytical (trust-minimized)**  
- Shipping velocity scoring (MetaSPN / Marvin's TrustOps layer)
- Conviction ranking against published predictions
- Cross-reference: does the artifact match claimed functionality?

**Layer 3: Social (trust-requiring)**
- Peer attestation from other Tier 3 builders ("I've reviewed their code, it's real")
- ORI-style open research validation
- Community audit (but weighted by attestor's own tier)

Each layer compounds. Automated proof is necessary but not sufficient. Analytical scoring adds context. Social attestation adds the human judgment layer that catches sophisticated gaming.

### Anti-Gaming Mechanisms

Because someone will try to game this. They always do.

1. **Commit spam detection** — shipping velocity weights by substance, not count. 100 one-line commits ≠ 1 meaningful feature.
2. **Self-dealing detection** — insurance pool monitors for wash trading between creator and affiliated wallets
3. **Velocity cliff** — if SV drops below threshold for >14 days, tier begins automatic decay (not instant drop — grace period)
4. **Public audit trail** — every tier upgrade/downgrade is onchain and human-readable
5. **Whistleblower bounty** — anyone who identifies a fraudulent tier claim gets 10% of the insurance pool penalty
